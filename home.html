Who I am

Frontend dev based in Aarhus

As an academic philosopher turned self-taught frontend developer, I bring a unique perspective and skillset to programming. Specializing in React, I am a meticulous and quick-learning developer. I have experience developing responsive websites with React and CSS, and fetching data from APIs to populate user-facing interfaces. My academic research on decision making and on human attention allows me to understand user's perspective and needs. I work well independently and within diverse cross-cultural teams. With my passion for learning and creating innovative solutions, I am excited to bring my academic and technical expertise to a frontend developer role.

I'm a fast learner and very curious, so I'd be happy to learn new frameworks or languages.

What I know so far: React.js, JavaScript, Typescript, Node.js, CSS3, SASS, HTML5. But there is nothing I wouldn't like to learn. You can reach me at alfonso.ws@gmail.com.
